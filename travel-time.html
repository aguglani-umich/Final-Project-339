Travel Time

Founder & Developer|December 2016 - December 2019

Travel Time tells you when to leave for the airport and gives you everything you need to never miss another flight

WHAT
Three years ago, I noticed that my parents would consistently miss their flights when they traveled for work. They would underestimate how long the lines were and were confused about the locations of their gates. Having loved airplanes ever since I was little, they started ask me: "When should I leave for the airport". I came up with an algorithm that considered everything from time of day, to airport size, to the type of flight they were getting on. I used this for them every time they traveled and in the meantime I started noticing that a lot of others faced the same problem. When I first learned how to code, I decided to build Travel Time to automate my makeshift algorithm so anyone who had this problem could use my app. From there Travel Time has grown to a full-service day of travel app that allows you to do everything from check flight status to buy food at the airport all in one place. Travel Time is used in 80+ countries and has touched over thousands of users over the years.
WHY?
I built Travel Time to make the travel experience for everyone, but more importantly to learn what it took to launch a product and build a business around it. I really got to understand what the design process was through my experience building Travel Time.
WHO
I started Travel Time and brought on Justin Waltrip as my Co-Founder in early 2017. At different points in our history our team varied from just the two of us to a team that was 6 strong. A majority of the development and design was done myself.
WHEN
